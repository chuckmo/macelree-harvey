About Us

Our 30+ attorneys and their staff are dedicated to providing clients with superior representation and are committed to achieving the best outcomes.

With roots that reach back to 1880, MacElree Harvey uses the traditional values of professionalism and excellence as the foundation for the firm’s identity. Our attorneys are encouraged to build on these traditional values through Initiative – creative problem-solving, proactive planning, and strategic positioning.

MacElree Harvey attorneys have long had a reputation for Initiative® – in listening, in counsel, in court, and in protecting the interests of every client.

It is a reputation earned from over a century of hard work and diligent service. It is a reputation that causes our clients to rely on us every day – and to look ahead with the confidence that we will be here for generations to come.

Whether your legal concerns encompass personal matters, your business, litigation, or even emerging areas of the law, MacElree Harvey is a firm where you will always find Initiative in Practice®. For every client. In every matter. Every day.

History of the firm

1880 Firm founded by Wilmer W. MacElree
1908 J. Paul MacElree joins the firm
1949 Lawrence E. MacElree joins the firm
1953 Lawrence MacElree appointed President Judge of the newly formed Orphans’ Court of Chester County
1954 John O. Platt joins and the firm name is changed to MacElree and Platt
… …
1970 Lichtenfeld and Gallagher merge with MacElree, Platt, Marrone and Harvey
Numerous name changes occurred over the years that follow:
  • MacElree, Harvey, Gallagher & Kean
  • MacElree, Harvey, Gallagher & O’Donnell
  • MacElree, Harvey, Gallagher & Featherman
  • MacElree, Harvey, Gallagher, Featherman & Sebastian, Ltd.
